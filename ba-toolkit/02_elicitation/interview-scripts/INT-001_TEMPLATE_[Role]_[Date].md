## Opening (5 min)

*[Read naturally — don't read verbatim]*

"Thank you for your time. I know it's valuable so I'll keep this focused. Our goal today is to
understand [specific domain] from your perspective — so we can make sure we build what you
actually need, not what we assume you need.

Can I start by asking you to walk me through how [relevant process] works today from your
point of view?"

*[Let them talk. Do not interrupt. Note what surprises you.]*

---

## Layer 1 — Explore: Current Reality (10 min)

*Goal: Understand the current state. Surface the real pain before jumping to solutions.*

1. "Walk me through your typical [day / week] when it comes to [domain]. What does that look like?"
   *[Probe: "Then what happens? Who does that? How long does that take?"]*

2. "What's the most frustrating part of how this works today?"
   *[Probe: "When does it break down? What do you do when it does?"]*

3. "What do you have to do manually today that you wish just happened automatically?"

---

## Layer 2 — Context (8 min)

*Goal: Understand why things work the way they do. Reveal constraints and history.*

4. "Why does it work that way? Was it always like this, or did something change?"

5. "Who else is involved in this process — upstream or downstream from you?"

---

## Layer 3 — Process (10 min)

*Goal: Get the exact step-by-step. Expose where the system touches their work.*

6. "Walk me through [specific task] step by step — from the moment you start to the moment it's done. What do you open, click, type, check?"
   *[Note every tool, system, and manual step they mention]*

7. "What information do you need that you don't currently have easy access to?"

8. "How do you know when something has gone wrong in this process? How do you find out?"

---

## Layer 4 — Future State (7 min)

*Goal: Understand what "better" looks like to THEM — not our version of better.*

9. "If this project goes perfectly — what does your day look like six months from now?
    What are you doing that you can't do today? What are you not doing anymore that you do now?"

10. "How would you know the new system is actually working? What would you measure or notice?"

---

## Hypothesis Check (5 min)

*Goal: Test our current understanding. Listen for corrections — corrections are the gold.*

"Based on everything you've told me, here's what I think you need: [state your current understanding clearly].

Does this sound right? What's missing? What's wrong?"

*[Write down every correction or addition. These are requirements.]*

---

## Clarity Check (2 min)

"On a scale of 0 to 10, how confident are you that I now understand what you need from this system?"

**Score received:** ____ / 10

*If < 8:* "What would move that number to an 8 or 9? What's the gap?"

**Gap identified:** [What they say is still unclear or missing]

---

## Close (3 min)

"Is there anything else about [domain] that you think I should know — that I haven't asked about?"

"Is there anyone else I should be talking to? Anyone whose perspective would be important to include?"

"I'll share session notes with you for review — is that OK? Any questions for me before we close?"
