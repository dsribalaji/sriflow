## Engagement Strategy

### Key Player (High Power, High Interest)
- **Sri (S1):** Keep closely engaged. Daily involvement. Final authority on all decisions.

### Must Be Satisfied (Medium Power, High Interest)
- **Managers (S2, S3):** Consult on approval workflows. Regular updates on budget features. Address their approval needs early.

### Keep Satisfied (Medium Power, Medium Interest)
- **Auditor (S6):** Ensure export features meet their needs. Quarterly check-ins on data format requirements.

### Keep Informed (Low Power, High Interest)
- **Members (S4, S5):** Regular updates on entry UX improvements. Gather feedback on category management and expense logging.
