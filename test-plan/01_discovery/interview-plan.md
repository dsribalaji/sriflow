## Interview 1 — Sri (Admin/Builder)

**Goal:** Resolve U1 — What's the minimum viable feature set for a 4-5 person team?

**Questions:**
1. What are the absolute must-have features for v1?
2. What features can wait for v2?
3. What's the data model in your head?
4. How do you envision the daily workflow?
5. What's the budget for this project (time, not money)?

**Expected Output:** Feature list with v1/v2 prioritization.

---

## Interview 2 — Managers (S2, S3)

**Goal:** Resolve U2 — How do managers approve budgets without creating bottlenecks?

**Questions:**
1. Walk me through your current budget approval process.
2. What information do you need to approve a budget?
3. How long should approval take?
4. What happens if you're unavailable?
5. What's your ideal approval workflow?

**Expected Output:** Approval workflow specification.

---

## Interview 3 — Members (S4, S5)

**Goal:** Resolve U3 — How easy is expense entry? Mobile support?

**Questions:**
1. Walk me through logging an expense today.
2. What information do you need to enter?
3. How often do you log expenses?
4. Do you need mobile access?
5. What's the biggest pain point in current expense tracking?

**Expected Output:** Entry UX requirements.

---

## Interview 4 — Auditor (S6)

**Goal:** Resolve U4 — What data do auditors need? Export format?

**Questions:**
1. What data do you need for a quarterly audit?
2. What format do you prefer (CSV, PDF, Excel)?
3. How do you verify data integrity?
4. What's your audit timeline?
5. Do you need real-time access or periodic exports?

**Expected Output:** Export specification.
