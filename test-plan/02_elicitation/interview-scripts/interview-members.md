# Interview Script — Members (S4, S5)

## Project: Personal Finance Tracker Web App
## Date: 2026-06-28
## Duration: 45 minutes
## Goal: Define expense entry UX

---

## Opening (5 min)

"Thanks for meeting. I need to understand how you log expenses and what makes it hard. No wrong answers — I need the reality."

---

## Layer 1 — EXPLORE (15 min)

### Q1: Walk me through logging an expense.
**Probe:** What tool do you use? How long does it take? What information do you enter?

**Expected Answer:** Manual entry, 2-3 minutes, date/amount/category/description.

### Q2: What hurts about the current process?
**Probe:** What's the most frustrating part? What do you avoid doing?

**Expected Answer:** Tedium, forgetting, inconsistent categories.

### Q3: How often do you log expenses?
**Probe:** Daily? Weekly? Monthly? What stops you from logging more consistently?

**Expected Answer:** Weekly batch, sometimes forget for weeks.

---

## Layer 2 — CONTEXT (10 min)

### Q4: Why does expense tracking matter to you?
**Probe:** Is this personal? Team? What's the business value?

**Expected Answer:** Team expense tracking, budget awareness.

### Q5: What information do you need to enter?
**Probe:** What's the minimum viable entry? What's optional?

**Expected Answer:** Date, amount, category, description. Receipt optional.

---

## Layer 3 — PROCESS (15 min)

### Q6: How do you categorize expenses?
**Probe:** Do you have predefined categories? How do you choose?

**Expected Answer:** Informal categories, pick whatever looks right.

### Q7: What does a good expense entry look like?
**Probe:** Walk me through the ideal entry experience.

**Expected Answer:** < 30 seconds, auto-categorize, smart defaults.

### Q8: Do you need mobile access?
**Probe:** How often are you on mobile vs desktop? What's the use case?

**Expected Answer:** Yes — logging on the go, receipts from phone.

---

## Layer 4 — IDEA/FUTURE (10 min)

### Q9: What would "better" look like?
**Probe:** If this app worked perfectly, what would your daily routine be?

**Expected Answer:** Quick entry, auto-categorization, mobile-friendly.

### Q10: What's missing from your current process?
**Probe:** What feature would make this a must-have for you?

**Expected Answer:** Receipt photo, voice entry, smart suggestions.

---

## Closing (5 min)

"What did I miss? What question should I have asked that I didn't?"

## Key Findings

1. **Current State:** Manual spreadsheet, tedious entry, inconsistent categorization
2. **Pain Points:** Tedium, forgetfulness, no mobile access
3. **Desired State:** Quick entry, auto-categorization, mobile-friendly
4. **MVP Features:** Quick entry, predefined categories, mobile-responsive, receipt photo
