# Interview Script — Sri (Admin/Builder)

## Project: Personal Finance Tracker Web App
## Date: 2026-06-28
## Duration: 60 minutes
## Goal: Define MVP feature set

---

## Opening (5 min)

"Thanks for meeting. I'm here to understand what you need from this finance tracker. I'll ask about your current process, what hurts, and what 'better' looks like. No wrong answers — I need the reality, not the ideal."

---

## Layer 1 — EXPLORE (15 min)

### Q1: What exists today for expense tracking?
**Probe:** Walk me through how you track expenses right now. What tool do you use? (Spreadsheet? Paper? App?)

**Expected Answer:** Likely manual — spreadsheet or paper receipts.

### Q2: What hurts about the current process?
**Probe:** What's the most frustrating part? What do you avoid doing because it's painful?

**Expected Answer:** Manual entry is tedious, categorization is inconsistent, no summaries.

### Q3: What's the reality of daily usage?
**Probe:** How often do you actually log expenses? What stops you from logging more consistently?

**Expected Answer:** Inconsistent — sometimes weekly batch, sometimes forget.

---

## Layer 2 — CONTEXT (10 min)

### Q4: Why does expense tracking matter to you?
**Probe:** Is this for budgeting? Tax prep? Personal awareness? What's the business value?

**Expected Answer:** Personal awareness + budget management.

### Q5: Who else is affected by your expense tracking?
**Probe:** Does your partner see this? Accountant? Anyone else?

**Expected Answer:** Just the team (4-5 people).

---

## Layer 3 — PROCESS (20 min)

### Q6: Walk me through logging an expense from start to finish.
**Probe:** What information do you need? How do you categorize? What's the minimum viable entry?

**Expected Answer:** Date, amount, category, description. Maybe receipt photo.

### Q7: How do you currently categorize expenses?
**Probe:** Do you have predefined categories? How many? Who manages them?

**Expected Answer:** Probably informal categories, need formalization.

### Q8: What does a "monthly summary" look like in your head?
**Probe:** What metrics matter? What would you look at daily/weekly/monthly?

**Expected Answer:** Total by category, budget vs actual, trend over time.

---

## Layer 4 — IDEA/FUTURE (10 min)

### Q9: What would "better" look like?
**Probe:** If this app worked perfectly, what would your daily routine be?

**Expected Answer:** Quick entry (< 30 sec), automatic categorization, visual summaries.

### Q10: What's missing from your current process that you wish existed?
**Probe:** Alerts? Reports? Integration? What feature would make this a must-have?

**Expected Answer:** Budget alerts, trend charts, maybe CSV export.

---

## Closing (5 min)

"What did I miss? What question should I have asked that I didn't?"

## Key Findings

1. **Current State:** Manual spreadsheet, inconsistent entry, batch processing
2. **Pain Points:** Tedium, forgetfulness, inconsistent categorization
3. **Desired State:** Quick entry, auto-categorization, visual summaries, alerts
4. **MVP Features:** Expense entry, categories, monthly summaries, budget alerts, CSV export
