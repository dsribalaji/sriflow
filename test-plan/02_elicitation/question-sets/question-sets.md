# Question Sets — Layered Design

## Project: Personal Finance Tracker Web App
## Date: 2026-06-28

---

## Stakeholder: Sri (Admin/Builder)

### Layer 1 — EXPLORE
1. What exists today for expense tracking? (Current state)
2. What hurts about the current process? (Pain points)
3. What's the reality of daily usage? (Usage patterns)

### Layer 2 — CONTEXT
4. Why does expense tracking matter to you? (Business value)
5. Who else is affected by your expense tracking? (Stakeholder scope)

### Layer 3 — PROCESS
6. Walk me through logging an expense from start to finish. (Workflow)
7. How do you currently categorize expenses? (Categorization)
8. What does a "monthly summary" look like in your head? (Reporting)

### Layer 4 — IDEA/FUTURE
9. What would "better" look like? (Desired state)
10. What's missing from your current process? (Feature gaps)
