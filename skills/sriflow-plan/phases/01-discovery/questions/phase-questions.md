# Phase 1 Questions — Discovery

## Step 1.1 — Confirm Project Context

**Q1:** What system or product are we building? (One sentence — concrete noun: API, UI, CLI tool, data pipeline, service)

**Q2:** What phase is this project in?
- A) Greenfield (no existing system)
- B) Existing system (enhancement/modification)
- C) Migration (moving from old to new)

**Q3:** Do we have any existing documentation?
- A) No documentation
- B) BRD draft exists
- C) PRD exists
- D) Design brief exists
- E) Legacy system docs exist

**Q4:** Has any stakeholder discovery been done before?
- A) No — starting fresh
- B) Yes — partial stakeholder list exists
- C) Yes — full register exists (skip to Phase 2)

---

## Step 1.2 — Identify Stakeholders

**Q5:** Who commissioned this work? Who is paying for it? (Name + title)

**Q6:** Who will use the system daily? (Name + title + role)

**Q7:** Who will use the system weekly or occasionally? (Name + title + role)

**Q8:** Who approves the work as "done"? Who signs off? (Name + title)

**Q9:** Who will be affected if this fails? (Name + title + how they're affected)

**Q10:** Who has blocked or paused similar work in the past? (Name + why)

**Q11:** Who has data or systems this project depends on? (Name + what they control)

**Q12:** Who is NOT in the room but should have a voice? (Name + why they matter)

---

## Step 1.3 — Power/Interest/Uncertainty Map

**Q13:** For [Stakeholder Name]:
- Power: High / Medium / Low (Can they stop the project?)
- Interest: High / Medium / Low (Does the outcome affect their daily work?)
- Top Uncertainty: What is the single biggest open question this stakeholder carries?

---

## Step 1.4 — Uncertainty Prioritization

**Q14:** Which uncertainties are Tier 1 (resolve this week)? List them.

**Q15:** Which uncertainties are Tier 2 (resolve before sprint start)? List them.

**Q16:** Which uncertainties are Tier 3 (resolve before build completes)? List them?

---

## Step 1.5 — Disagreement Diagnostic

**Q17:** Are there any vague phrases that multiple stakeholders are using differently? (e.g., "better visibility", "smarter reporting", "more efficient")

---

## Step 1.6 — Interview Plan

**Q18:** For each Tier 1 stakeholder, confirm the interview plan:
- Who: [Name]
- Goal: [What uncertainty to resolve]
- Duration: [30/45/60 min]
- Preferred format: [In-person / Video / Phone]
