## Resolution Tracking

For each uncertainty:
1. **Current State:** What do we know?
2. **Gap:** What don't we know?
3. **Resolution Method:** How will we find out?
4. **Owner:** Who is responsible?
5. **Deadline:** When must this be resolved?
