# Interview Script Template

## Pre-Interview Setup
- **Stakeholder:** [Name]
- **Date/Time:** [Date]
- **Duration:** [30/45/60 min]
- **Goal:** [What uncertainty to resolve]

---

## Opening (2 min)
- Thank them for their time
- State the purpose: "I'm here to understand how you currently handle [process] and what challenges you face."
- Assure confidentiality
- Ask permission to take notes

---

## Layer 1 — EXPLORE (10 min)
1. "Walk me through how you currently handle [process]."
2. "What parts of this process are most frustrating?"
3. "How much time do you spend on this weekly?"

---

## Layer 2 — CONTEXT (8 min)
4. "Why does the process work this way today?"
5. "Who else is involved in this process?"

---

## Layer 3 — PROCESS (15 min)
6. "Let's walk through a specific example step by step."
7. "What happens when something goes wrong?"
8. "What tools do you use at each step?"

---

## Layer 4 — IDEA/FUTURE (10 min)
9. "If you could change one thing about this process, what would it be?"
10. "What would success look like for you?"

---

## Closing (5 min)
- Summarize what you heard
- Ask: "Did I miss anything important?"
- Confirm next steps
- Thank them
