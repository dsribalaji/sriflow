## Question Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Description | Weight |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Layer Correct | Question matches the layer's purpose | 20% |
| Open-ended | Not yes/no, invites elaboration | 20% |
| Role-appropriate | Calibrated to stakeholder's level | 20% |
| Uncertainty-targeted | Directly addresses a Phase 1 unknown | 20% |
| Assumption-free | No embedded solutions or leading | 20% |

**Minimum acceptable average: 7/10 per question**
